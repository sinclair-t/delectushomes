Close Protection Services

At Delectus, we offer discreet and professional Close Protection services for high-profile individuals, executives, celebrities, and VIPs. Our highly trained security personnel provide overt or covert protection, ensuring safety without intrusion. Whether you require one-to-one protection or a full security team, we tailor our services to meet your specific needs.

With years of experience in high-risk environments, our team conducts comprehensive risk assessments, identifying potential threats and implementing strategic security measures. From event security to international travel protection, Delectus ensures unmatched safety and discretion at all times.
