About our Security

Excellence in Security

At Delectus Security Agency, we specialize in providing expert security solutions across multiple sectors, including real estate, healthcare, events, leisure, and personal protection. With years of industry experience, our highly trained professionals are committed to ensuring safety, reliability, and peace of mind for our clients. We take a proactive approach to security, utilizing advanced technology and strategic planning to mitigate risks and protect people and assets. Whether securing large-scale events, safeguarding healthcare facilities, or offering tailored close protection services, Delectus is your trusted partner in security.

FAQ's

Answers to your Questions

What types of security services does Delectus offer?

We provide a wide range of security solutions, including installation, keyholding, matchday event security, leisure security, NHS healthcare security, and close protection services.

Are your security personnel licensed and trained?

Yes, all our security professionals are fully trained and certified to meet industry standards, including SIA licensing, to ensure top-tier protection and service quality.

Can you provide security for large-scale events?

Absolutely. Our event security services include crowd management, stewarding, bag checks, incident response teams, and more to ensure the safety of all attendees.

Where are Delectus apartments located in the UK?

Our premium apartments are located in key cities across the UK, offering modern, secure, and fully serviced accommodations.

Are the apartments available for short-term and long-term stays?

Yes, we offer flexible leasing options, including both short-term and long-term rentals to suit your needs.

How do I book an apartment with Delectus?

You can browse available apartments on our website and contact us directly to book or schedule a viewing.

Delectus provides expert security solutions across various sectors and offers premium, modern apartments in the UK, ensuring safety, comfort, and reliability for all clients.
